Course Details

UTKARSH

One Year Classroom programme for Class X students.
Course Name UTKARSH
Eligibility Class X appearing students
Who Should Join All students who aspire to crack IIT JEE(Main and Advanced), NTSE, olympiads and excel in Board exams.
Course Commencement MARCH-MAY
Course Duration 42 weeks

ABOUT THE COURSE

In this course the student gets entire exposure to the syllabus of Class-X (CBSE, NTSE, Olympiads curriculum) and important topics of Class-IX . Once the complete syllabus is completed revision session of is conducted.

Course Structure

The complete syllabus of Class X is covered in 2 phases
PHASE SCOPE
PHASE 1(March-May to December) Covering entire Class X syllabus.
PHASE 2(Revision - December to March) Revision and Doubt clearing session for X exams.
